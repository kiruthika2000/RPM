About

BUILT ON RELIABILITY.
DRIVEN BY SPEED.

I'm a Product Manager specializing in payment infrastructure — the systems that move money into, out of, and within financial platforms. Deposits, withdrawals, internal transfers, ACH rails, wire networks, card integrations, and the data aggregators and payment processors that make it all work.

At FINRA, I defined product requirements for payment rails and vendor integrations on a platform processing 75B+ daily transactions — partnering with engineering, compliance, and legal to deliver robust, reliable, regulatory-compliant fund movement. At Cognizant, I scaled payment infrastructure for 12+ Fortune 500 financial services clients, reducing transaction failures and latency while evolving infrastructure to support new products.

I'm wired for urgency. Payment systems don't get second chances — every transaction failure is a customer who didn't get their money. I monitor success rates, processing times, and operational overhead relentlessly, and I bring the same standard to building as to measuring.
